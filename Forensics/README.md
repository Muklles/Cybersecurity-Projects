# Forensics Projects

Projects developed as part of my digital forensics learning, focusing on investigative techniques, evidence analysis, and practical application of cybersecurity concepts.

## Project List

### [Caso 1 - Vandalismo](./Caso%201%20-%20Vandalismo)

A case study based on a local vandalism investigation involving eggs thrown in the neighborhood, where police questioned a suspect and analyzed device logs.

**What I learned:**
- Utilized device logs to reconstruct suspect activities
- Applied digital forensics techniques for minor crime analysis
- Learned about evidence correlation and verification
- Practiced documenting findings clearly for investigative purposes

---

### [Caso 2 - Mitchell Jones](./Caso%202%20-%20Mitchell%20Jones)

A digital investigation of a homicide case that occurred in the USA, analyzing evidence and reconstructing the timeline of events.

**What I learned:**
- Applied investigative methodology to a homicide case
- Collected and analyzed digital and physical evidence
- Built a timeline of events to understand the sequence of the crime
- Developed reporting skills for law enforcement context
